About Nick

Nick Smeriglio is a New York–based actor, stand‑up, creator, and podcast producer. Born in Arlington Heights, IL, he left corporate life in 2021 and has since performed at venues like Caroline’s on Broadway, Governor’s, and Zanies. On screen and air, he’s appeared on Fox News and Fox 5 NY, produced the all‑Italian‑language podcast Cresciuto Italiano, and made his film debut in Statistical Probabilities, earning Best Supporting Actor.
